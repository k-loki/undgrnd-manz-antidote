# The Beginning of Infinity

> Notes from an endless pursuit of better explanations.

This repository powers my personal blog — a place to think in public, refine ideas, document mistakes, and explore questions that seem worth taking seriously.

Topics you'll find here include:

* Artificial Intelligence
* Software Engineering
* Startups & Leverage
* Physics
* Economics
* Evolution
* Psychology
* Philosophy
* History
* Books, Anime & Storytelling
* Anything sufficiently interesting

## Why this exists

Writing is one of the best ways I know to expose fuzzy thinking.

This blog is less a portfolio and more a companion in an ongoing attempt to understand how things work, stress-test assumptions, and leave behind explanations that are hopefully a little better than the ones I started with.

I don't expect to arrive at final answers.

I am more interested in learning how to ask better questions.

Perhaps this is my small contribution to what David Deutsch calls *The Beginning of Infinity* — the idea that knowledge can grow without bound, and that there may always be a better explanation waiting to be discovered.
