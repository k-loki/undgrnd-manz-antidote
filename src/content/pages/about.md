I am Loki!

This blog is more of a companion for an unending journey in my quest to understand the world better.
A place to think in public, refine ideas, document mistakes, and chase better explanations.

I might write about software, startups, artificial intelligence, physics, economics, history, philosophy, books, anime, and whatever else captures my attention long enough to deserve careful thought.

I am more interested in learning how to ask better questions.
Don't expect to arrive at final answers.


Perhaps this could be my small contribution to what David Deutsch calls *The Beginning of Infinity* — the idea that knowledge can grow without bound, and that there may always be a better explanation waiting to be discovered.

If you're curious, ambitious, skeptical, or just enjoy wandering through difficult ideas, Welcome aboard.

We're all temporary observers trying to make sense of an absurdly large universe. We might as well leave behind good notes.
